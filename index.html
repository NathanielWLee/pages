About Me

I am a professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University and a fellow member of the ACM, IEEE, and SMA. Prior to joining OSU, I spent two years as a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Before that, I received my Ph.D. from Purdue University, Master's at the Indian Institute of Science Bangalore, and Bachelor's at Jadavpur University in Kolkata.
